How to play

  • Choose difficulty level (easy/medium/hard) and press START button. Click on the cards and match as many pairs in quickest amount of time with least number of flips
  • For EASY, time limit is 60 seconds, you score a minimum of 10 points each pair. For MEDIUM, time : 50 seconds, points: 20. For HARD, time : 40 seconds, points: 30.
  • BONUS points is given based on the amount of time that is currently remaining, so the quicker you pair up the cards the higher that BONUS is. For Example, if on the EASY level, you have just matched a pair while there is 50 seconds left, then you will score 10+(10*50) = 510 points
  • WIN by matching all the cards before the clock runs out, your final score is deducted by the number of times you flipped cards over.
